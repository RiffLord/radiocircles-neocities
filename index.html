odd radio circles

An Electronic Music Project from Herzegovina

introduction

Hello, World! Odd Radio Circles is an electronic music project from Široki Brijeg (Bosnia & Herzegovina), active since 2020.
Exploring much the same sonic territory as Transmissions, from which the project evolved, Odd Radio Circles blurs the lines between electronica, ambient, minimalism and drone.
Building atmospheric instrumental tracks from a foundation of steady beats supporting layers of synthesizers, field recordings, guitars, and any other source of sound available, the music of Odd Radio Circles is evocative and abstract, rooted in nostalgia for the sounds and places of the past while reconstructing them into something out of time.
