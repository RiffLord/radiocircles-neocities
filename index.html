odd radio circles

Hello, World! Odd Radio Circles is an electronic music project from Široki Brijeg (Bosnia & Herzegovina), experimenting with electronica, ambient, drone music and minimalism.
The project began in 2020 as a direct continuation of Transmissions. The change, though not necessary (the music stayed the same), was due to a need for renewal.
The music of Odd Radio Circles is at times abstract and at times evocative, usually based on a foundation of slow and steady beats sourced from a vintage drum machine, with layers of synthesizers, field recordings and occasionally guitars rounding out the sound.
