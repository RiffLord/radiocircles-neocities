odd radio circles

An Electronic Music Project from Herzegovina

biography

Odd Radio Circles is an electronic music project by Bruno Pezer. Hailing from the small town of Široki Brijeg in Bosnia & Herzegovina, Bruno spent his childhood between his homeland and various countries abroad, settling in the sleepy coastal city of Brindisi in the South of Italy where he spent his formative years.

He first approached music through the guitar and bass, playing alone and in various short-lived local groups. Always nurturing a fascination for the atmospheric, otherworldly, and sometimes surreal sounds that only synthesizers could make, his interest in electronic, ambient, and experimental music grew over the years, eventually resulting in his first attempts at writing and producing under the moniker Transmissions.

His music is a blend of ambient-electronica atmospheres, steady beats, minimalism, drone, psychedelia, and spacey synth-pop melodies.

His first releases were for the independent record labels Triple Moon Records, on which he made an appearance in a compilation, and Sounds Against Humanity with which he produced two albums. A further EP and full length were self-released, with some sporadic live activity and collaborations with other musicians taking place.

The 2020 pandemic and resulting national quarantine in Italy brought with it a need for change, and though stylistically following the same direction, Transmissions was reborn as Odd Radio Circles. The first track for the new project was commissioned for a program on an independent radio station in Louisiana, USA. Odd Radio Circles' first album, Stories in the Dust was reworked from unreleased Transmissions material in collaboration with the poet Simon Chiatante and released in 2021. The next couple of years brought an EP and a few experimental singles resulting from further collaborations, including appearances on Chelidon Frame's Asynchronous Drone Orchestra. In the meantime, work was slowly but surely taking place on the follow-up full-length, Concerto for Fish, originally conceived as a Transmissions album in 2018.

Odd Radio Circles currently resides in Berlin, Germany, and is always developing ideas for further releases.
